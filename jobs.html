Work at Packfleet

Help us deliver the future of commerce

It’s easier than ever to start a business online. Entrepreneurs across the UK are testing out side-projects, selling their hobby creations, and starting the next-generation of big businesses. But traditional delivery services are stuck in a pre-internet world, making it impossible to compete against Amazon.

We’re on a mission to empower independent businesses across the UK to offer the best deliveries on Earth, saving them time, money and stress, and making their customers the happiest they can be.

Our success is directly tied to the success of our merchants. When they grow, we grow. And so we’re here to help them do that by offering delivery infrastructure that makes their customers more likely to choose them, and more likely to return again and again. And all while accelerating the green transition for commerce and proving that it doesn’t need to come at the expense of our drivers.

We’re just getting started. We’re a small team with huge ambitions and we need your help to make them a reality. We’re looking for smart, driven people who want to be part of shaping what we build and take ownership across the business.
