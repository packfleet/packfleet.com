About Packfleet

We’re on a mission to empower independent businesses to offer the best deliveries on Earth

We started Packfleet because we thought there must be a better way.

Whether it was deliveries that never turned up or parcels “hidden” in our recycling bins, it always felt like a system that was built for a past century.
In a world where it’s never been easier to start a business, the legacy couriers just aren’t up to scratch. So we’re here to change that.

We're starting by offering our service to small and medium sized businesses in London, working with them to build the perfect delivery service. And then we'll look to grow from there.
