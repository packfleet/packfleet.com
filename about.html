We started Packfleet to build the best courier company

It all started with wine. Our founder, Tristan, runs a wine subscription company called Winepost. And while trying to find the best way to send that wine to customers, he tried as many couriers as he possibly could.

And none of them were great. Missing packages, random delays, poor customer service (for both us and our customers), expensive fees, and old-fashioned technology tended to be the norm. One delivery was once delivered into a wheelie bin!

And that's where Packfleet started. We figured out that we could deliver packages better and cheaper than the traditional courier companies and offer it to other small and medium sized businesses in London. We're small now, but that means we can provide an amazing service to both you and your customers. And we've never delivered a parcel into a wheelie bin...
